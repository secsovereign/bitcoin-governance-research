02:43 < wangchun> what are ANCESTOR/DESCENDANT limits? My tx failed the test.
02:46 < sdaftuar> wangchun: starting in 0.12, there are limits on the length of unconfirmed transaction chains. "ancestors" refers to all the unconfirmed transactions that a given transaction depends on
02:46 < sdaftuar> wangchun: "descendants" refer to the unconfirmed transactions that depend on a given one
02:47 < sdaftuar> these limits apply to mempool acceptance of new transactions
