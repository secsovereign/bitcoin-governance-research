14:40 <sipa> For the rest, either FindBestAncestorSet (for ancestor set feerate quality) or FindBestCandidateSetEfficient (for optimal linearization) is invoked to find good subsets.
14:42 <sipa> And there are two helper data structures in other files, FeeFrac (an exact fee/size ratio) and BitSet (for representing subsets of clusters efficiently).
