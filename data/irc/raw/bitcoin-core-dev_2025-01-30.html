16:32 <darosior> Resources are also spread inequally. It's very hard to onboard and make meaningful impact in important areas. 16:32 <darosior> Bitcoin Core cannot be the "everything Bitcoin app" without also diluting priorities. Not doing anything about it is making this choice by default.
